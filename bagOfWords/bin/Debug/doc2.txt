FICO® Entity Resolution Engine adds a critical dimension to the fight against fraud by resolving the true identities of perpetrators, and proactively revealing fraud rings. In numerous industries –– financial, healthcare, insurance, government and more –– FICO Identity Resolution Engine significantly reduces fraud by accelerating the discovery of highly sophisticated, and organized, financial criminals
